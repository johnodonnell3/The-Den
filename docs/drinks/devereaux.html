Devereaux

Ingredients

  • 1 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 oz St. Germain elderflower liqueur
  • 1/2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 3 oz sparkling wine

Instructions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add bourbon, St. Germain, lemon juice, and simple syrup.
  2. Strain into an ice-filled glass.
  3. Top with sparkling wine and garnish with mint leaf
